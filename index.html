5 killed, several missing as picnic trawler capsizes in Padma

At least five people were killed and several others went missing when a boat carrying 46 people capsized after a
collision with a bulkhead in a tributary of the Padma river in Munshiganj tonight.

The incident took place around 8:30pm when a sand-laden trawler hit the boat near Subchani Bazar in Autshahi union of Tongibari upazila, reports our Munshiganj correspondent quoting Louhajang Fire Service and Civil Defence Station Officer Qayes Ahmed.
